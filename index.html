Croissant

Croissants are one of the most delicious pastries that came from Austria. They are buttery and flakey with so many layers of lamination. To make mouthwatering croissants, it takes lots of patience, and dedication. Various pastry cehfs around the world have made their own variations from the classic croissant. For example, the cronut is a croissant-donut hybrid.
